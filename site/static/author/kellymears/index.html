Thanks for taking action to kick Big Pharma out of government!

Pharma makes more profit than any other industry on Earth, and Americans pay higher prices for medicines than any other country on Earth. All because our government has allowed drug corporations to establish monopolies on drugs developed with taxpayer-funded research.

Our next President must get serious about breaking up with Big Pharma. Because life and health should not be for sale.

It’s Time To Change The Conversation Around Big Pharma

The American people are the biggest funders of medical research and innovation through our tax dollars. Yet prescription drug corporations have spent millions lobbying for monopoly prices on the same life-saving medicines we paid to invent!

We demand the next president guarantee that the medicines invented on the taxpayer’s dime cost no more in the U.S. than in other wealthy nations, and that these drugs are available off-patent for poor countries everywhere.
